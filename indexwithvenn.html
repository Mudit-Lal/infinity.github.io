Future of Tech Education

Oct 16 | 5:30 PM to 8:30 PM (IST)

Eminent academicians discuss why we need a new approach to tech education and the current global best practices in the field. Especially useful for those who are tirelessly working to make the world a better place and are curious to learn how reinventing tech education can support their endeavours.


Tinker. Create. Solve.

Oct 17 | 10:00 AM to 12:30 PM (IST)

Tech-based solutions to real-life problems need more than academic prowess. This track focuses on the importance of design, entrepreneurship and social context in creating impactful solutions. Join if you identify yourself as a tinkerer, problem-solver, dreamer, creator or an innovator.


Tech Leaders for Tomorrow

Oct 17 | 6:00 PM to 8:45 PM (IST)

This track brings together tech leaders and entrepreneurs of today who discuss emerging, cutting-edge technologies, how to lead an organization and build a start-up. Recommended for those who are exploring career options, building careers or creating their own ventures.


Stories which inspire!

Oct 18 | 5:30 PM to 8:00 PM (IST)

Hear outstanding individuals share their journey of grit and determination. Inspirational stories ranging from building institutions which put students in the forefront, to innovatively working towards a more inclusive world. For anyone who believes technology education needs to be reimagined, wants to use technology to create an impact or become a tech leader.
