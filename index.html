Reinventing Tech Education

Unprecedented events of the past few months have brought the infinite possibilities of technology to the forefront. To convert these infinite possibilities into impactful solutions to society’s biggest challenges requires learning and innovation. The starting point are temples of learning - our schools and universities.

Infinity 2020 brings together global technology leaders and eminent academicians to discuss the most cutting edge ideas to reimagine technology education. It is a platform for meaningful dialogues, to push boundaries of the way we think about technology education, and reinvent it for the need of the future.
